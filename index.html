Commission Structure

Simple, transparent terms

Two engagement types, same rate. You earn 10% — we handle everything else.

Project
10%
of total contract value, paid when the client's first payment is received.
Retainer
10%/mo
of monthly retainer value, paid every month for the first 6 months as the client pays.
Example

You refer a company that signs a 100-hour/month retainer at $15,000/mo.
Your commission: $1,500/month for 6 months = $9,000 total.
For a $25,000 project: $2,500 one-time payment.

Terms

How it works

  • No minimum deal size.
  • Open to anyone — clients, peers, friends, advisors.
  • The referral must be a new client (not an existing Brightbase client).
  • A qualifying referral is an introduction that leads to a signed engagement.
  • Commissions are paid via check, Venmo, Zelle, or wire — your preference.
  • No cap on the number of referrals you can make.
  • This program can be modified or discontinued at any time with notice to active referral partners.
